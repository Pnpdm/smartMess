The Problem

Why do hostel messes waste so much food?

Inaccurate Headcount

Mess managers don't know how many students will actually come for each meal. Food is prepared based on guess-work.

No Visibility

Student preferences and attendance patterns are not tracked, leading to repetitive menus and low student satisfaction.

Waste of Resources

Extra food often lands in the dustbin instead of reaching NGOs or shelters that need it urgently.
